About Me

Junior Alabi (pronounced Ala-Bee) is a champion salsa dancer who performs around the globe, including over 75 countries throughout 6 Continents. Junior has choreographed for So You Think You Can Dance, FOX TV, Disney Channel TV shows and been featured on several television specials. He has guest appeared on Dancing With the Stars Italy, China's #1 TV Show "Up Junior" and the Kris Jenner Show. People may recognize Junior Alabi from his recent work in the finals of America's Got Talent.
